## Script

Never watched a single Chinese anime? These five are the perfect first step, no homework required. One: Mo Dao Zu Shi, gorgeous, emotional, and it actually ends. Two: Link Click, if you want something modern and grounded before diving into fantasy. Three: Battle Through the Heavens, for straightforward hero-gets-stronger fun. Four: A Mortal's Journey to Immortality, once you're ready to slow down and really savor it. Five: Scissor Seven, if you want short, weird, and genuinely funny. The biggest myth about donghua is that you need to know Chinese mythology to enjoy it. You don't. You just need one good entry point, and any of these will do it. So which one belongs at number one on this list? Tell me where I got the order wrong.
