## Script

So you want to get into donghua, but every series has three hundred episodes and you have no idea where to begin. Relax. Here's the no-spoiler way in. If you want the easiest entry, start with Mo Dao Zu Shi: a tight mystery with a real ending. If you want cultivation done right, A Mortal's Journey to Immortality is the gold standard, just be patient, it rewards you. If you want hype and fast power-ups, Battle Through the Heavens never slows down. One rule: don't jump straight into the thousand-episode epics until something hooks you first.
