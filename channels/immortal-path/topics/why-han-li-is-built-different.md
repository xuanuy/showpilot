## Script

Han Li might be the least flashy protagonist in all of cultivation, and that's exactly what makes him terrifying. No noble bloodline, no destined romance, no heaven-sent cheat code. He starts as a poor village kid who joins a sect mostly so he can eat better. So how does he reach immortality? Paranoia. Han Li assumes everyone is lying, every treasure is a trap, every ally will eventually betray him, and he is usually right. He hides his real strength for decades, runs from fights he could win, and only strikes when survival is guaranteed. While other heroes chase glory, Han Li chases longevity. That's the entire philosophy. He doesn't want to be the strongest, he wants to be the last one breathing. Is patience the most underrated power in xianxia? I think so. What about you?
