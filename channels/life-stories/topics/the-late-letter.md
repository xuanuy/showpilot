## Script

A busy son kept meaning to call his aging mother back, but work always came first. Later, he told himself. There will always be time. One afternoon a letter arrived in her shaky handwriting. It simply said, I am not writing because I need anything. I am writing because I miss the sound of your voice, and I wanted you to have something to keep. He smiled, set it aside, and promised to call that weekend. He never got the chance. The weekend came with different news. For the rest of his life he carried that letter in his coat, a reminder that love postponed is love we gamble with. Call the people who would drop everything for you. Do it before later becomes too late. Who are you going to call today? Tell me below.
