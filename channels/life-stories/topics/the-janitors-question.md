## Script

A struggling student stayed late at school every night, hiding the fact that home was not a safe place to be. The old janitor never pried. He just asked the same quiet question each evening as he mopped the halls: did anything good happen to you today? At first the boy mumbled, nothing. But the man kept asking, gently, every single night. Slowly the boy started searching his days for one good thing, just to have an answer. A kind teacher. A song. A passing grade. Years later that boy, now a teacher himself, ended every class with the same words. Did anything good happen to you today? Sometimes saving someone isn't dramatic. Sometimes it's just refusing to stop asking. Has one small question ever changed your life? Share it below.
