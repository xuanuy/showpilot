## Script

There was a man at the office everyone overlooked. He never spoke in meetings, ate lunch alone, and went home the moment the clock hit five. People assumed he just didn't care. Then one day he didn't come in, and the whole floor slowly fell apart. The printer no one knew how to fix stayed broken. The new hires no one had time for felt lost. The little problems he had quietly solved for years suddenly had no one. He had been holding the place together without ever asking for credit. When he finally returned, the office threw him a small lunch. He cried. He thought no one had noticed him at all. Quiet people are often carrying more than we see. Who is the quiet one holding your world together? Go thank them today.
