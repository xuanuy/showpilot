## Script

It was pouring rain when a woman noticed a soaked teenager standing frozen at the bus stop, no umbrella, clearly afraid to walk home in the storm. Without thinking, she handed him hers and said, just keep it. She walked the rest of the way drenched, certain she would never see that umbrella again. Years later, at her new job, a young man froze when he saw her, then quietly placed an umbrella on her desk. It was the same one. He said he had carried it through college as a reminder that a stranger once cared whether he made it home safe. The smallest kindness can outlast everything. You may never see where it lands.
