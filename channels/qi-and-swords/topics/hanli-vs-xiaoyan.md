## Script

Han Li versus Xiao Yan. Two of the biggest names in cultivation, and fans argue about this constantly, so let's settle it. Xiao Yan is raw firepower: the Pure Lotus Demon Flame, Buddha's Fury Lotus, dragon transformations. He overwhelms you with pure offensive pressure. Han Li is the opposite. He almost never fights fair. Poison, sword formations, spirit beasts, escape arts, a backup plan for his backup plan. Put them at the same realm and Xiao Yan wins the first exchange, easily. But Han Li doesn't fight to win exchanges. He fights to make sure you never walk away. My take? In a straight duel, Xiao Yan takes it. In a battle to the death, I'm betting on Han Li every single time. Who's got it? Comment your pick and tell me why.
