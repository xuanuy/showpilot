## Script

Mo Dao Zu Shi is the donghua that proved cultivation stories could be about something other than getting stronger. At its core it's a murder mystery wrapped in reincarnation. Wei Wuxian dies branded as the greatest villain of his age, comes back years later in a stranger's body, and piece by piece we learn that the man everyone hated might have been the only one who was right. The animation is stunning, Tencent clearly spent the money, but the reason it went global is the writing. It's about how the righteous crowd can be the real evil, and how having principles can get you killed. And the bond between Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji carries the entire thing. So if you only watch one donghua to understand the hype, is this it, or would you pick something else?
