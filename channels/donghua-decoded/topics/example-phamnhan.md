## Script

Here's the cultivation donghua everyone sleeps on, and that's a mistake. A Mortal's Journey to Immortality isn't about a chosen one. Han Li has zero talent, zero bloodline, zero cheat. What he has is patience, paranoia, and a refusal to die. Every power-up he earns, he earns the ugly way: scheming, hiding, surviving fights he should lose. That's why it hits different. Most xianxia heroes win because the plot loves them. Han Li wins because he out-thinks people who are stronger than him. The animation in the latest arcs is genuinely top tier, but the real hook is that the weakest guy in the room keeps walking out alive. So tell me, is Han Li the smartest protagonist in all of cultivation, or am I overrating him? Drop your pick.
