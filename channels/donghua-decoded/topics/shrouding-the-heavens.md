## Script

Shrouding the Heavens opens with a bronze coffin floating in space and a group of college classmates dragged into the path of immortality, and somehow it only gets stranger and better from there. This is the donghua for people who think they've seen every cultivation story. It fuses hard sci-fi mystery with classic Daoist immortality: ancient stars that turn out to be tombs, emperors who simply refuse to die. Ye Fan isn't a chosen one handed power on a plate; he's an ordinary man who decides the universe's rules are negotiable. The scale is what hooks you. This isn't one sect fighting another, it's a man trying to cheat death itself across entire eras. If you want xianxia with a genuine mystery box, this is the one. What donghua would you tell a total beginner to start with?
