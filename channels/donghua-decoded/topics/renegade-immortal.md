## Script

Renegade Immortal might be the darkest cultivation story ever animated, and that is exactly why you should watch it. Wang Lin is not a hero. He's a talentless village kid clawing his way up through a world that would happily grind him into dust, and to survive he becomes someone willing to do almost anything. There's no plot-armor kindness here, no fated romance that saves the day. Just a man who decided that staying alive is worth any price. What makes it hit so hard is that the story never lets you forget the cost of that choice. Every ally lost, every line he crosses, it all stays with him. This is xianxia written for adults. So here's my real question: is Wang Lin a tragic hero, or did the cultivation world just create a monster? I honestly can't decide.
