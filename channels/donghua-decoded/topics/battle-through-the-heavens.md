## Script

Battle Through the Heavens has run for years, and the Pure Lotus Demon Flame arc is where it stops being a revenge story and becomes something bigger. Here's what people miss about Xiao Yan: he isn't the strongest, he's the most relentless. He started as the genius who lost everything, the kid his whole clan wrote off. Now he's walking straight into the Soul Palace's trap, facing mind-controlled flame servants and a fire that thinks for itself. What makes this arc land is the cost. Every flame he swallows, every level he climbs, he gives up a piece of who he was. That's the trade most xianxia refuses to show honestly. So is Xiao Yan still the underdog we rooted for, or did the power quietly change him? Tell me where you stand.
