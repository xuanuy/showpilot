## Script

Donghua does not get enough credit for how stunning it looks now, so here are the series with the most jaw-dropping animation. Perfect World turned its recent arcs into full cinematic spectacles, every fight basically looks like a movie. A Mortal's Journey to Immortality nails the atmosphere, those quiet, eerie cultivation worlds genuinely feel alive. And the Renegade Immortal movie pushed 3D donghua to a level that honestly embarrasses some big-budget films. The studios in China leveled up so fast that the animation alone is a reason to watch, before you even fall in love with the stories.
