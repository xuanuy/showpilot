## Script

If you're new to donghua, here are the rookie mistakes that make people quit before it gets good. Trap one, starting with a thousand-episode series first, you'll drown, begin with something shorter. Trap two, judging the early episodes too fast, a lot of donghua start slow then completely explode. Trap three, watching low-quality reuploads with terrible subs instead of the real thing. Trap four, expecting Japanese anime pacing, donghua tells stories its own way, slower but deeper. Avoid these four and you'll actually fall in love with the genre instead of bouncing right off it.
