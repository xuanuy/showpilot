# Organic growth playbook (no paid ads)

The pipeline already handles the mechanical wins. This file is the **manual** part —
the behavior that actually grows a Reels page. Do these in priority order.

## What the pipeline already does for you
- ✅ Vertical 1080×1920, burned captions (most people watch muted → captions = watch time)
- ✅ Hook line in the first 3 seconds (the script's opening)
- ✅ CTA question in both the voiceover **and** the caption (drives comments)
- ✅ Niche + broad hashtag mix
- ✅ Consistent daily posting via cron (20:00 SGT, 1/page/day)

## Tier 1 — biggest levers (do these every post)
1. **Reply to every comment within the first hour.** Comments + your replies = 2× the
   engagement signal. This single habit moves reach more than anything else.
2. **Watch-time is king.** Keep videos 25–45s, no slow intro. The hook must make them
   stay past 3s. If a video flops, it's almost always the first 2 seconds.
3. **Pin a question as the first comment** yourself right after posting (same as the
   caption CTA) — seeds the comment section so others pile on.
4. **Post at peak + be consistent.** Cron covers consistency. Don't skip days; the
   algorithm rewards regularity more than volume.

## Tier 2 — compounding habits
5. **Engage as the Page** on bigger donghua pages/reels (genuine comments) before and
   after you post — pulls their audience to your profile.
6. **Reuse winners.** When one reel pops, make 2–3 follow-ups on the same series/angle
   immediately while it's hot. Ask Claude to write them.
7. **Cross-surface:** share each reel to the Page's Story too (manual, 5s in the app).
8. **Series hooks:** "Part 1 / Part 2" framing makes people follow to catch the next one.

## Community engagement (the right way — seeds real "mồi")
Run `python3 engage.py` each day — it prints a 15-min checklist + 6 genuine comment
starters (rotates daily through `engagement/comment_bank.json`). Adapt each line to the
actual reel before posting. Want fresh ones for a specific video? Ask Claude.

Do this from ONE real account, 15–20 min/day:
- Comment something genuine on 5–10 trending donghua reels from OTHER creators. Curious
  people click your profile → your Page. This is real marketing, not manipulation.
- Join 2–3 big donghua/xianxia Facebook Groups; answer questions, start discussions.
  Don't drop bare links (reads as spam).
- The Page auto-commenting its own CTA (already automated) is fine and expected.

**Do NOT** create clone/extra accounts to comment on your own Pages, and do NOT use a
browser bot to auto-comment on other people's posts. Both are inauthentic/automated
behavior: Facebook links accounts by device/IP/fingerprint, flags scripted activity as
spam, and cascades bans to the Pages too. AI-generated drive-by comments are also low
quality and get reported. The only durable engagement is genuinely human.

## Tier 3 — measure & iterate
9. Check **Professional Dashboard → Insights** weekly. Sort reels by reach + watch time.
   Find the pattern in your top 3 (series? hook type? length?) and make more of that.
10. Kill what doesn't work. If an angle gets no traction after 3 tries, drop it.

## Hard rules (protect the page)
- ❌ No bought views / followers / bot engagement — instant trust penalty or ban.
- ❌ No reposting other creators' edits — only official trailers/PV under your commentary.
- ❌ Don't post the *same* video to multiple Pages — make each page's script distinct.

## Realistic timeline
New pages sit flat for 2–4 weeks, then a single reel breaks out and pulls the rest up.
Don't judge before ~30 posts. Consistency is the whole game early on.
